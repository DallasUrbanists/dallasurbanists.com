Hyperlocal Conversations

Neighborhood-specific monthly gatherings where neighbors explore ways to improve walkability where they live.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a hyperlocal conversation?

Foster informed and empathetic conversations about walkability, transportation, and housing in Dallas neighborhoods. Through stories and conversations, inspire locals to take action toward meaningful improvement for the city.

What happens at a hyperlocal conversation?

Dallas Urbanists began as a blog in December 2021 by Hexel Colorado, documenting his advocacy with groups like Dallas Neighbors for Housing and Dallas Bicycle Coalition. Hexel’s Instagram videos offered tips on using DART, living car-free, and covered key urban planning issues.

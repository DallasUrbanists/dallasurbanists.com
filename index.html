Hyperlocal Conversations

A hyperlocal conversation is a neighborhood-specific monthly gathering where neighbors explore ways to improve walkability where they live. Our vision is to have a hyperlocal conversation in every neighborhood in Dallas.

Learn more and get involved with a conversation near you!

Bimonthly Urbanists Mixer

Every two months, neighbors and advocates from across the city gather downtown to fellowship, exchange ideas, and get caught up on what's happening in Dallas. Our regular meeting place is in The Underground at Frankie's Downtown.

Learn more and get ready for our next mixer!

What is Dallas Urbanists?

We are a nonprofit created for and by people who care about making our city a better place to live, work, and play for everybody. We align with the New Urbanism movement, and we are an instance of what Strong Towns calls a "Local Conversation", whose purpose is to connect nearby, like-minded advocates to put Strong Towns ideas into action where we live.

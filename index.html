What is Dallas Urbanists?

Dallas Urbanists is a website (dallasurbanists.com) and a social media channel (@dallasurbanists on Instagram) featuring advocacy and educational content about walkability, transportation, housing, and urban planning. Created by Hexel Colorado in December 2022, it aims to amplify ideas and voices for a more walkable, bike-friendly, transit-oriented, affordable, sustainable, safe, accessible, equitable, and vibrant Dallas. Learn more about the who, what, when, where, and why of Dallas Urbanists.

History of Dallas Urbanists STLC

Dallas Urbanists began as a blog in December 2021 by Hexel Colorado, documenting his advocacy with groups like Dallas Neighbors for Housing and Dallas Bicycle Coalition. Hexelâ€™s Instagram videos offered tips on using DART, living car-free, and covered key urban planning issues.

Since then, Dallas Urbanists has become a dynamic community presence, featured by KERA Radio, Dallas Morning News, WFAA TV, and other media outlets. We have gained over 15,600 Instagram followers, collected thousands of signatures for transit and pedestrian safety, and partnered with local groups to support affordable housing and bike infrastructure.

In October 2024, Dallas Urbanists took its first steps toward becoming a Strong Towns Local Conversation (STLC).

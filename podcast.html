About

{% include impact-image.html path = "assets/podcast-impact-photo.jpg" %}

By Way of Dallas is a podcast about mobility in the Dallas area, and how the way we move affects the way we live. This podcast primarily targets transportation planners and advocates for public transit, cycling infrastructure, and walkability.

For planners, this podcast sheds light on how people are helped and hurt by Dallas' infrastructure policy decisions. From personal stories shared by Dallasites to interviews with local experts and advocates, this podcast aims to shatter the myth that car dependence and sprawl are Dallas' inescapable fate.

For advocates, this podcast presents ways toward a more walkable and sustainable Dallas. Topics will include the removal of Interstate 345, investment in sidewalk gaps and cycling infrastructure, eliminating parking minimums, and more.

You can listen to By Way of Dallas on every major podcast platorm: Spotify, Google Podcasts, Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music, iHeartRadio, Castbox, Pocket Casts, RadioPublic, and Stitcher.
